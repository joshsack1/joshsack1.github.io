Joshua Sack

I am a PhD student in the economics at American University. My research interests include agent-based modeling and its applications to inequality, taxation, and cooperation. Before starting my degree, I worked on a number of Democratic campaigns as an opposition researcher. In my free time, I support Liverpool FC, and like to run long distances in very thin shoes.
